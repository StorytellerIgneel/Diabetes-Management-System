Management of T2DM

At diagnosis of T2DM the following should be performed: 
› detailed history and physical examination, focusing on key issues which will affect treatment decision 
› baseline investigations to assess ASCVD* risk factors and complications of T2DM

Management is based on the results of the above. 
Management involves lifestyle modification, medications and patient education encouraging self-care and empowerment.
    * atherosclerotic cardiovascular disease
