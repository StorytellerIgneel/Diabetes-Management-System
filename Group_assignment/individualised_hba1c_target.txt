Individualised HbA-1c targets based on different patient profiles
≤ 6.5 %
•	Newly and recently diagnosed
•	Younger age 
•	Healthier (no complications) 
•	Low risk of hypoglycaemia

6.6 % - 7.0 %
•	Normal circumstances

≥ 7.0 %
•	Elderly patients 
•	Presence of co-morbidities 
•	High risk of severe hypoglycaemia; hypo unawareness (unable to tell when blood glucose level drops to dangerous levels)
•	Short life expectancy
